## West Bengal Government Corruption — Complete Overview

### Major Scams (Chronological)

| Scam | Year | Amount | Status |
|------|------|--------|--------|
| **Saradha Group Ponzi** | 2013 | ₹2,000-3,000 crore ($4-6B) | CBI investigating, multiple arrests |

### 1. Saradha Group Financial Scandal (2013)

Largest Ponzi scheme in Eastern India. Saradha Group (200+ companies) collected ₹2,000-3,000 crore from 1.7 million depositors before collapsing in April 2013.

Key People:
- Sudipto Sen — Chairman, arrested in Kashmir
- Debjani Mukherjee — Executive Director (former receptionist)
- Kunal Ghosh — TMC MP, CEO of Saradha Media
- Srinjoy Bose — TMC MP
- Madan Mitra — TMC Transport Minister
- Mithun Chakraborty — Bollywood actor, brand ambassador

Political Nexus:
- TMC MPs received salaries from Saradha Group
- Madan Mitra headed employees' union, encouraged public to invest
- Sudipto Sen bought Mamata Banerjee's paintings for ₹1.8 crore
- Mamata's government issued notification for public libraries to buy Saradha newspapers
- Money laundered to Dubai, South Africa, Singapore via Jamaat-e-Islami Bangladesh

Aftermath:
- ~210 agents/depositors/executives committed suicide
- Supreme Court transferred investigation to CBI (May 2014)
- Multiple TMC ministers arrested
- CBI interrogated Kolkata Police Commissioner Rajeev Kumar for 39 hours

### 2. Rose Valley Financial Scandal (2013)

Ponzi scheme by Rose Valley Group, collected ₹17,500 crore from 25+ lakh depositors. More massive than Saradha but less politically exposed initially.

Key People:
- Gautam Kundu — Rose Valley Group Chairman, arrested by ED
- Multiple TMC politicians allegedly received funds

### 3. Narada Sting Operation (2014/2016)

Journalist Mathew Samuel recorded high-ranking TMC officials accepting cash bribes on camera in exchange for political favors.

Caught on Camera:
- Mukul Roy — TMC General Secretary (now BJP)
- Suvendu Adhikari — TMC MP (now BJP)
- Subrata Mukherjee — TMC Minister
- Firhad Hakim — TMC Minister
- Sovan Chatterjee — TMC MLA
- Madan Mitra — TMC MLA
- Rajeev Kumar — Kolkata Police Commissioner (then)

### 4. West Bengal SSC Teacher Recruitment Scam (2022)

Massive corruption in teacher recruitment through West Bengal School Service Commission (WBSSC). Thousands of appointments made illegally through bribery and political connections.

Key People:
- Partha Chatterjee — Former Education Minister, arrested by ED (July 2022)
- Arpita Mukherjee — Close associate, ₹50 crore cash found at her residence
- Multiple WBSSC officials arrested

### 5. Coal Smuggling Scam (2020-2024)

Illegal coal mining and smuggling in Eastern Coalfields Limited (ECL) areas. Mafia controlled coal mines, extracted coal worth ₹1,300+ crore.

Key People:
- Anup Majhi (Lala) — Coal mafia kingpin, key accused
- Jyotipriya Mallick — TMC MLA, arrested by ED (October 2023)
- Vinay Mishra — TMC youth leader, absconding

### 6. Sandeshkhali Violence (2024)

Systematic land grabs, extortion, sexual violence, and political mafia rule in Sandeshkhali (North 24 Parganas).

Key People:
- Sheikh Shahjahan — TMC local strongman, arrested after months of hiding
- Shibu Hazra — TMC leader
- Uttam Sardar — TMC leader

### Political Context

Ruling Party: All India Trinamool Congress (TMC) since 2011

Pattern:
- Ponzi schemes flourished under political patronage
- Central agencies (CBI/ED) used by BJP-led central government against TMC
- TMC alleges "political vendetta" by central agencies
- BJP uses corruption as major campaign issue
- Supreme Court has intervened multiple times
