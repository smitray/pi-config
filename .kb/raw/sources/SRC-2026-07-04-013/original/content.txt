## Indian Fraud/Scam Reporting Process

### Step 1: File a Complaint

| Type | Where to File |
|------|---------------|
| Cyber fraud / online scam | https://cybercrime.gov.in (National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal) |
| Financial fraud | Local police station (FIR) |
| Bank/payment fraud | Your bank + police FIR |
| Big scam (₹1+ crore) | Economic Offences Wing (EOW) |

### Step 2: Gather Evidence

Before filing, collect:
- Screenshots of messages/transactions
- Bank statements / UPI transaction IDs
- Phone numbers / emails of scammer
- PAN number — include in complaint
- Any contracts/agreements
- Timeline of events

### Step 3: File Online (Easiest)

Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

1. Click "Report Cyber Crime"
2. Select "Financial Fraud" or "Online Scam"
3. Fill details: what happened, amount lost, scammer info
4. Upload evidence
5. Submit — you'll get a complaint number

### Step 4: Also Report To

| If... | Report to |
|-------|-----------|
| Lost money via bank | Your bank (for chargeback/dispute) |
| Lost money via UPI | NPCI portal (upihelp.com) |
| Lost money via wallet | Wallet company (Paytm, PhonePe, etc.) |
| Scammer is minor (<18) | Juvenile Justice Board / Child Welfare Committee |
| Scammer is adult (18+) | Regular criminal court |

### Juvenile vs Adult Court

- Juvenile court (Juvenile Justice Board) handles crimes committed by minors (under 18 years)
- PAN cards are issued to individuals 18+ years old
- If someone has a PAN, they're likely an adult, not a juvenile

### Anti-Corruption Agencies

| Agency | Role | Website |
|--------|------|---------|
| CBI | Central investigation | https://cbi.gov.in |
| ED | Money laundering | https://enforcementdirectorate.gov.in |
| Income Tax | Tax evasion | https://www.incometax.gov.in |
| ACB | State anti-corruption | State-specific websites |
| Cyber Crime | Online fraud | https://cybercrime.gov.in |

### Quick Action Steps

1. Go to https://cybercrime.gov.in
2. File complaint with all details
3. Also file FIR at local police station for paper trail
4. Report to bank/payment platform for chargeback
5. Keep complaint number safe for follow-up
